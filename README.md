# Neutral Zero

**Cooperative Asteroids with P1/P2 Factions and AI Co-pilot.**

## Features
- **Two Factions**: Vanguard (VYKOS) and Remnant (RAELUN).
- **AI Co-pilot**: Play solo with an AI ally or in 2-player local co-op.
- **GFX Modes**: 
  - **Vectrex**: Retro vector lines.
  - **Normal**: Modern glow and effects.
  - **RTX**: High-end shaders, bloom, and post-processing.
- **Co-op Mechanics**: Ships share a shield when in close proximity.

## Narrative
The Vykos and the Raelun have been at war for ninety years. Stranded in a rogue asteroid field, they must cooperate to escape.
- **Win**: The war ends that day.
- **Lose**: It continues for forty more years.

## Controls
- **P1**: `WASD` to move, `Space` to fire.
- **P2**: `Arrow Keys` to move, `Shift` to fire.
